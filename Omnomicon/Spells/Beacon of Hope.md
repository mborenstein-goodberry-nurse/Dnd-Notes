### [[Beacon of Hope]]

Source: Player's Handbook

*3rd-level [[abjuration]]*

**Casting Time**: 1 action
**Range**: 30 feet
**Components**: V, S
**Duration**: [[Concentration]], up to 1 minute

This spell bestows hope and vitality. Choose any number of creatures within range. For the duration, each target has [[advantage]] on **[[Wisdom]] saving throws** and [[Death Saving Throw|Death Saving Throws]], and regains the maximum number of [[hit points]] possible from any healing.
